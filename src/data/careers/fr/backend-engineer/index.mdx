## About the Role

As a Backend Engineer at Starter, you'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the server-side components that power our applications. You'll work closely with frontend developers, data engineers, and DevOps specialists to create scalable and reliable systems.

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement efficient, maintainable backend services and APIs
- Optimize database queries and data access patterns for performance
- Collaborate on system architecture and technical decision-making
- Ensure high availability, reliability, and security of backend systems
- Write clean, well-tested code with comprehensive documentation
- Participate in code reviews and mentor junior engineers

## Why Starter?

- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package
- Flexible work arrangements with remote options
- Continuous learning opportunities and professional development budget
- Collaborative, inclusive work environment with talented peers
- Opportunity to work on challenging problems at scale
- Regular team events and company-wide activities

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
- 4+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong knowledge of backend technologies and best practices
- Experience with version control systems and CI/CD pipelines
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
