## About the Role

As a Data Scientist at Starter, you'll be responsible for analyzing complex datasets to extract actionable insights that drive business decisions. You'll work closely with product, engineering, and business teams to identify opportunities for data-driven optimization and innovation.

## What You'll Do

- Analyze large, complex datasets to identify patterns and trends
- Develop and implement statistical models and machine learning algorithms
- Create clear, compelling data visualizations and dashboards
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define metrics and KPIs
- Communicate findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Stay current with the latest developments in data science and machine learning

## Why Starter?

- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package
- Flexible work arrangements with remote options
- Access to cutting-edge tools and technologies
- Collaborative environment with autonomy to explore new approaches
- Professional development opportunities and conference attendance
- Regular team events and company-wide activities

## Requirements

- Master's degree or PhD in Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
- 3+ years of professional experience in data science
- Strong programming skills and experience with data analysis libraries
- Experience with statistical analysis and machine learning techniques
- Excellent communication skills and ability to explain complex concepts
- Proven track record of delivering actionable insights from data
